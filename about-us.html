Our Mission

Transforming the 'paper chase' into an intellectual journey.

We centralize quality preparation materials to empower COMSATS students with clarity and authority in their academic pursuits.

The Story Behind COMSATSPrepHub

Founded by Mushtaq Ahmad Saqi, COMSATSPrepHub was born out of a simple yet profound frustration: the fragmented and unreliable nature of academic materials.

What began as a personal archive of vetted notes grew into a mission to build a central repository where quality is never compromised for quantity. Saqi envisioned a platform that doesn't just provide answers, but fosters a deeper understanding of the curriculum.
